Travel

I enjoy travelling to unique destinations several times a year - most recently I visited the following places:

Costa Rica

There were many things to enjoy in Costa Rica including diverse wildlife, tropical forests at high elevation and wonderful hospitality.

Arapaho and Rosevelt National Park

This area of Colorado near Estes Park featured lots of trails to hike and enjoy the mountain scenery.

Bandalier National Monument

This part of New Mexico near Los Alamos was a site of settlements by ancestors of Puebloan peoples dating back to the 11th century. There were many canyons and caves to explore.

Grand Canyon National Park

This famous park probably needs little explanation - it was clear why the Grand Canyon is such a popular travel destination.
